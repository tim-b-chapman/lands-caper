Three ways we work

Art. Hardscapes. Garden.

Most landscapers do one of these. We do all three — and they work better together.

01 — Art
Where the space becomes something

This is the part most landscapers skip. Custom tile-work, resin inlays, hand-laid mosaic paving, murals, sculpture — the details that make a space feel intentional rather than installed.

  • Custom paving & tile-work
  • Resin inlays
  • Murals
  • Sculpture & found-object features
  • Creative space design
02 — Hardscapes
Built to last. Built to look the part.

Steel, stone, brick, corten — structural elements that age well instead of looking tired. Corten steel develops a rust patina over time that only gets better. The hardscape is the bones of the space.

  • Steel frame decks
  • Paving & stonework
  • Brickwork & tiling
  • Corten steel (rust patina finish)
  • Reclaimed & salvaged materials
03 — Garden
The living part.

Planting that suits the soil, the sun, and the person who has to live with it. Irrigation that actually works. Turf that handles Melbourne summers. The green stuff that ties everything else together.

  • Planting (natives, exotics, edibles)
  • Irrigation systems
  • Soil prep & improvement
  • Turf & lawn
